Who are we?

PhineX is a group founded by @A.Elgohary in 13/11/25. instead of programming alone taking months to build a project we decided to build a group of senior programmers to build projects faster and better that makes more profit than working alone.

We scout for the best programmers in our X-Academy and transform them into skilled developers to join our developers group. If you're a senior programmer, join our developers team to build more projects and grow PhineX into a major platform.

Our name represents the mythical phoenix - symbolizing transformation, rebirth, and rising to new heights. Just as the phoenix rises from its ashes, we help programmers transform their careers and reach new levels of success.
